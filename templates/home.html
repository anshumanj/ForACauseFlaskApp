About Us

FOR A CAUSE provides a charity fundraising ecosystem to connect the users of Amazon voice-service enabled devices and the charities wanting to raise donations.

We allow users to discover and donate to the causes that speak to them (pun intended!) via AmazonPay, while making it easier for the charities to raise donations.

This site is designed for the charities to register with us, so their information can be used on our Alexa voice skill.

How Do We Help Charities?

Charities register with us below and their information gets stored with us. Any donations raised by our Alexa Skill app gets collected to our AmazonPay account and we distribute out the donations to the the respective charities periodically. This allows the charities to raise funding via a new venue without creating any extra work upfront.
